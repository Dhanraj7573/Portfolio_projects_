About Me

I’m an analyst working in finance, mainly living in the world of revenue checks, audits, financial and investor reporting, and keeping clients and business teams in the UK, Ireland and APAC in the loop. Over time I’ve realised what I actually enjoy is untangling messy data – organising it properly, understanding what it’s really saying, and turning it into a clear story instead of just another spreadsheet. That’s what pulled me into Python, SQL and dashboards like Power BI: finding better ways to structure the data, make processes less painful, and get insights that people can actually use.

Excel – Bank Financial Analysis & ROE Decomposition

This workbook breaks down a bank’s return on equity into its key drivers over time. Using an extended DuPont-style tree, it separates leverage, asset returns, net interest income, non-interest income, provisions, tax rate and growth, showing how each factor contributes to overall ROE across multiple years.

Excel – Amazon Valuation Model

A full Amazon equity valuation built in Excel, combining company overview, historical and forecast financials, revenue mix and trading multiples with a DCF/SOTP valuation to arrive at an implied share value and investment view.

Excel – Renewable Energy Solar Financial Model

A project-finance model for a utility-scale solar plant. It forecasts revenue, expenses, EBITDA and cash flows, builds a detailed sources & uses schedule and capital stack, and calculates returns such as IRR, equity cash yields and payback.
